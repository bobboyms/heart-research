# Clinical Heart Murmur Studies

Selecao refeita com foco medico/clinico, excluindo trabalhos de inteligencia artificial, machine learning e classificacao automatica por fonocardiograma.

1. `1997_jama_does_this_patient_have_abnormal_systolic_murmur.pdf`
   - Does This Patient Have an Abnormal Systolic Murmur?
   - Tipo: revisao clinica baseada em exame fisico, serie Rational Clinical Examination.
   - Fonte: https://ucsdim.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/12/JAMA-Systolic-murmurs.pdf

2. `2017_auscultation_while_standing_pathologic_murmur_children.pdf`
   - Auscultation While Standing: A Basic and Reliable Method to Rule Out a Pathologic Heart Murmur in Children
   - Tipo: estudo clinico prospectivo em criancas encaminhadas por sopro.
   - Fonte: https://www.annfammed.org/content/annalsfm/15/6/523.full.pdf

3. `2018_heart_murmurs_echocardiography_findings_normal_newborn_nursery.pdf`
   - Heart Murmurs and Echocardiography Findings in the Normal Newborn Nursery
   - Tipo: coorte retrospectiva neonatal sobre achados ecocardiograficos em recem-nascidos com sopro.
   - Fonte: https://www.techscience.com/chd/v13n5/39058

4. `2022_diagnosis_cardiac_murmurs_children_vessel_plus.pdf`
   - Diagnosis of Cardiac Murmurs in Children
   - Tipo: revisao medica sobre diagnostico clinico de sopros cardiacos pediatricos.
   - Fonte: https://www.oaepublish.com/articles/2574-1209.2021.105

5. `2022_heart_murmurs_children_evaluation_management_aafp.pdf`
   - Heart Murmurs in Children: Evaluation and Management
   - Tipo: revisao clinica para avaliacao, manejo e encaminhamento.
   - Fonte: https://www.aafp.org/afp/2022/0300/p250
